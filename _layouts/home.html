About

I am Assistant Professor of English at the University of Miami. I research and teach at the intersections of cultural and media studies, contemporary US literature, and the digital humanities. My book is Training for Catastrophe: Fictions of National Security after 9/11 (University of Minnesota Press, 2021). I'm also a co-director of WhatEvery1Says, a project that employs machine learning techniques to examine contemporary public discourse about the humanities on a large scale.

Find me on Twitter, on Github, or via email.
